Who We Are

Welcome to Pacesetters Foundation, where we don’t just talk the talk; we walk the walk! We are a Christian faith-based foundation based in the vibrant city of Lagos, Nigeria, and we are on a mission to transform lives through mentorship, support, and scholarships. We believe that every student has the potential to be a superstar, and we’re here to help them shine!

Our programs are designed to be exciting yet profoundly impactful! From mind-blowing conferences that help reshape minds, to hands-on training sessions on key 21st century skills, we equip teens and youths with the tools they need to discover their purpose and make choices that will propel them into greatness. We’re not just about academics; we’re about building character, strong spiritual foundation and empowering dreams!

Join us in our quest to create a generation of leaders who posses strong moral character and faith, and are not afraid to dream big and take action. At Pacesetters Foundation, we’re all about making a difference, one student at a time. So, buckle up and get ready for an adventure that will change your life!

Have Questions? Find Answers Here!

What is Pacesetters Foundation about, & what's your mission?

We are Christian faith-based foundation that empowers students through mentorship and scholarships. Our mission is to promote spiritual and academic excellence among young people, fostering a community of bright minds dedicated to growth and success.
LEARN MORE

How can I get involved?

You can join our live events or volunteer with us, or support our mission by donating or spreading awareness, empowering us to continue empowering bright minds.

Who can apply for scholarships?

Students in universities (or any higher institution of learning) who have been mentored and monitored by our mentors.

What events do you organize?

We hold in-person conferences, hands-on trainings on key skills, and impactful workshops for our students and mentees.

How does the foundation help students?

The foundation offers financial assistance to bright students who lack funds for higher education, ensuring no talent goes unnoticed; in addition to providing ongoing mentorship and training.
